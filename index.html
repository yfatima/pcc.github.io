Patriot Cloud Conference is all-women and nonbinary conference, hosted by Society of Women Engineers (SWE) at George Mason University. In response to COVID-19, we will be hosting PCC virtually to keep our attendees healthy!

Over the duration of 5 hours, binary and non-binary people are immersed in newly adpoted tech culture and encouraged to learn to use of cloud computing tools such AWS and more.
