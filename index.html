George Mason University is having its first ever virtual Patriot Cloud Computing Conference on November 7th from 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM EST. This is being organized by the Society of Women's Engineers George Mason Chapter and the Institute of Digital InnovAtion.  We are anticipating over 500+ participants for this conference. We are working with top Cloud Computing providers such as Amazon Web Service and Microsoft Azure.

The Patriot Cloud Conference provides university students, faculty with insight into the emerging field of Cloud Computing. Cloud Computing has revolutionized the industry by allowing groundbreaking speeds of computer processes. It is being integrated in a variety of business models to accelerate their progress and success as cloud provides the computational power at low cost and high scale. Therefore, it is an essential knowledge to learn as a student, faculty or professional in the industry today.

  • Do I need to be a student to attend?
      Yes. Only students who are currently enrolled at a college/university or have graduated within the past 12 months are eligible to attend. Must also be 18 years or older. All disciplines are welcome!
  • How much does it cost?
      Nothing YAY! Attending Patriot Cloud Conference is free.
  • What should I bring?
      Since this event is all virtual, you dont't need to bring anything except yourself.
  • Do I need to have prior knowledge about cloud computing?
      No, not at all! This conference is for students with any level of knowledge about cloud computing. Just come with an open mind to learn about cloud computing and its services provided by tech companies.
  • Is registration required?
      Yes, it is encourged that you register before coming to the event. It will be open a week or so before the actual event. Register Now!
  • Where can we ask more questions?
      A discord server is in making for more questions and help before and during the event. Join our discord.
